Does money buy happiness in our century?



The project focuses on exploring the intricate relationship between GDP per capita, happiness, and life satisfaction. It aims to understand how income influences individuals' subjective well-being and to provide insights for policymakers. By delving into this topic, the project seeks to uncover the factors beyond monetary wealth that contribute to overall happiness and inform the development of effective policies to enhance the quality of life for citizens.
