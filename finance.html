Financial Analysis of the US stock market 2014-2018: How to diversify the stock portfolio? 



Stock markets are vital to economic growth, influencing sectors from education to technology. Market analysis, considering financial, political, and economic factors, helps in creating profitable portfolios. Various techniques have been used over the years to analyse the stock market, and this work used k-means clustering of the technology sector stocks to maximize the diversification of the portfolio and create the most profitable portfolio.
